Connect Google Photos

Enter your Google OAuth Client ID to connect your Google Photos library. Photos will be browsed directly from your account.

How to get a Client ID:
1. Go to console.cloud.google.com
2. Create a project → Enable "Photos Library API"
3. Credentials → Create OAuth 2.0 Client ID (Web)
4. Add this page's URL to authorized origins
5. Paste the Client ID above
